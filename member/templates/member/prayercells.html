What are prayercells?

This are just but sub sections of the common prayer and interaction channel.

How does it work?

They are all specifically and uniquely designed throughout the week across different residence. Members then meet to the agreed home, share a word, a meal if possible and a prayer.

Where to meet next?

During every Sunday service, an announcement is made concerning the prayer cells, in which the number of attended members is made clear and the next home to visit. 

How to join?

Joining is quite easy, just identify the area of residence among the list of prayer cells areas and join the one that is near you. If the available ones aren't satisfactory to you, you can always join the general prayer cell held in the main church premises
